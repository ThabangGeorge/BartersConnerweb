All About us

BartersConner was born out of personal challenge that the founder had as a Student, the challenge
of acquiring expensive textbooks that was prescribed for him.


From interaction with other students who had sold him their textbook he quickly came to the realization
that the reason why the students sold their textbooks was to help subsidies the cost of the newly
prescribed textbooks, It was from this that the idea of BartersConner was born the
simple idea of creating a central platform which would allow students to exchange/ trade
their textbooks for other textbooks using the sites digital credits
as a carrier of principle between the students .

Mofi Credits Checkmate.


Mofi Credits are the sites digital currency which will be use as a medium of exchange on the site , this will be used as a way to hold principle for the user of the platform and would be required for any exchange that will be executed on the site.

The reason for having a digital currency on the site is to have a dedicated credit for exchanging textbook, unlike money which has diverse use and due to that creates some conflict in the intended use in this case being acquiring textbooks the student can be assured that as long as they have the sites credit they will have textbooks.
Using coins will enable us to effectivly manage the exachanges between students which will mean that returning students will not be required to pay any cost beside the required subcription fee
